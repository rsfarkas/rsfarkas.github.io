    Design Question

    How can we use wearable technology to help keep women safer?

    Concept

    Guardian is a wearable product and mobile application that addresses the need to make women safer.

    It utilizes both extant mobile and wearable technologies, aiming to establish a mobile-friendly social network and emergency system that combats and prevents sexual assault and robberies against women.

    Implementation

    Guardian is a concept idea. A promotional website was created in order to pitch the idea.
